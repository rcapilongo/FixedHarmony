About Fixed Harmony

Here at Fixed Harmony, we create podcast reviews of our favorite undiscovered YouTube artists that we hope you will also enjoy. Our team is comprised of music and film industry professionals. We search through the millions of covers already posted on YouTube and connect you to covers that are actually worth watching. Be sure to check out our podcasts and listen to our educated opinions. Also be sure to follow our social media pages. Links to these can be found at the bottom of the page.
